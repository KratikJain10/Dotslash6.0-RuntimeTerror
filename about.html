Our Story

It all began from painting a wall in my college when I found that art is not just my hobby ,it is something that satiates my soul.Deep down I felt a very strong urge to express myself through art and make every soul realize the beauty of art.

Creating beauty is my passion and converting every imagination into reality is my goal.I want the world to feel the art as I do.And this let to the creation of Gingerart where I customise your dreams into pieces through my creativity

The story behind the logo is...We had a very loving cat in our hostel which died by falling through the rooftop.So I decided to keep gingercat as the logo and gingerart as name,it's a kind of tribute to our loving millo..
